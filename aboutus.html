About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting donors with those in need of blood donations. Our mission is to ensure that everyone who needs a blood transfusion can get one. We believe that a single donation can potentially save up to three lives.

Since our establishment in 2023, we have facilitated over 10,000 successful blood donations and have grown to a network of over 1,000 active donors. We are committed to making a difference in our community and invite you to join us in this life-saving mission.
